Stress is tied to the modern way of life — the constant chase for achievements, the dictation of joy and beauty from social media that imposes impossibly high standards, suggesting that anything less than perfect looks, unforgettable outings, and fascinating photos from expensive trips is unacceptable. Employers set targets that leave no time to enjoy reaching them, constantly pushing us to climb higher and work even harder.

Over time, stress becomes our daily companion. If we ignore it, it will escalate and manifest through various forms such as outbursts of anger, obsessive-compulsive patterns, panic attacks, and it will also endanger your physical health.

By applying modern knowledge and techniques, we will work to help you understand the context in which stress appears, its meaning and function in your specific case, and to strengthen your resilience to the various factors that cause it — so that you can control it rather than it controlling you, and so you can finally feel free and relaxed.
